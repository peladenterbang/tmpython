ICT Concepts Explained

Weekly Bias (PWH/PWL):

Previous Week High (PWH)
Resistance level - price may reject or break
Previous Week Low (PWL)
Support level - price may bounce or break
Bullish Bias: Price above PWL, targeting PWH
Bearish Bias: Price below PWH, targeting PWL

Order Blocks (OB):

Last opposing candle before impulse move
  • Bullish OB: Last red candle before up move
  • Bearish OB: Last green candle before down move
  • Entry when price returns to OB zone

Fair Value Gap (FVG):

Imbalance in price (3-candle pattern)
  • Gap between candle 1 high and candle 3 low
  • Price tends to return and fill the gap
  • Entry when price retraces to FVG

Kill Zones (Best Trading Times):

  • Asian: 00:00-04:00 UTC (accumulation)
  • London: 07:00-10:00 UTC (breakout)
  • NY: 12:00-15:00 UTC (continuation)

Power of 3 (AMD):

Daily price cycle:
  1. Accumulation: Asian session range
  2. Manipulation: False breakout (stop hunt)
  3. Distribution: Real move direction

Trading Days:

  • Monday: Avoid - manipulation day
  • Tuesday-Thursday: Best reversal days
  • Friday: Caution - weekly close
Tip: Wait for price to sweep a key level (liquidity grab), then look for OB/FVG entry in the opposite direction.
